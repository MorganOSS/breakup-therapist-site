Quick Answers

A few common questions.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes — I see clients virtually anywhere in Utah, as well as in-person at my Murray office.

What if I've never done therapy before?

That's completely fine. Most of my clients are new to therapy — we'll go at your pace, and I'll explain anything that feels unfamiliar.

How do I know if IFS is right for me?

You don't have to know going in. We'll talk through what you're experiencing at the consultation, and I'll recommend an approach from there.
