Core Service

Individual Breakup & Divorce Therapy

This is where most people start. There's no timeline you're supposed to be on — we'll go at the pace your nervous system can actually handle.

Depth Work

IFS Therapy (Internal Family Systems)

IFS is the foundation of how I work. Instead of treating your anxiety, anger, or shutdown as problems to eliminate, we'll get to know them as parts of you — each with a job, a fear, and a story. As we build trust with those parts, you'll likely notice you feel less at war with yourself, and more able to respond to hard moments instead of being run by them. This work goes beneath "coping skills" into the actual architecture of why you feel what you feel.

Learn more about this service →
Patterns

Anxious Attachment & Relationship Patterns

If you find yourself anxious in relationships — overanalyzing texts, fearing abandonment, losing yourself to keep someone close — we'll trace that pattern to where it started, usually long before this relationship. Understanding your attachment style isn't about blame. It's about giving you real choice in how you show up next time.

Grief

Grief & Loss Therapy

Losing a relationship is a legitimate loss — of a person, a future, a version of yourself — even though there's no funeral and few people checking in on you a month later. This work makes room for that grief fully, without rushing you toward "moving on" before you're ready.
