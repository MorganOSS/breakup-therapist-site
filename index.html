You're Not Overreacting

If any of this sounds familiar, you're exactly where you need to be.

Breakups don't just hurt your heart — they hijack your nervous system, your sense of identity, and your ability to trust your own judgment. Here's what that can look like:

The Replay Loop

You can't stop rerunning the relationship — searching for the moment it broke, the thing you should have said, the version of you that could have made them stay.

The Anxious Body

Your phone feels like a lifeline. You check it, check their socials, check your own reflection for proof you're okay. Your chest is tight and your sleep is gone.

Grief That Won't Follow a Schedule

You feel fine for a day, then it flattens you again. Grief doesn't move in a straight line, no matter how many people tell you it should.

The Bigger Questions

Underneath the immediate pain, you're asking harder ones: Who am I without this? Will I always attract this pattern? Can I actually trust myself?

None of this means something is wrong with you. It means something mattered — and parts of you are working hard to protect you from feeling that loss all at once. That's where we start.
How I Can Help

Support that meets you where the pain actually lives.

Every person's heartbreak is specific. Together, we'll figure out what yours needs — using an approach that goes beneath the surface, not just around it.

Breakup & Divorce Recovery

Whether it ended yesterday or a year ago, we'll work through the shock, the identity loss, and the practical weight of rebuilding — at a pace that respects where you actually are.

IFS Therapy

We'll get curious about the different parts of you — the anxious part, the angry part, the part that still hopes — and help them work together instead of against each other.

Anxious Attachment & Relationship Patterns

If you keep ending up here — anxious, overgiving, afraid of being left — we'll trace the pattern to its root and build a different way of loving and being loved.

Grief & Loss

Losing a relationship is a real loss, even when no one died. We'll make space for that grief instead of rushing you past it.

LGBTQ+ Affirming Therapy

You deserve a therapist who understands your relationship and identity without having to translate yourself first. This is a space built for that.
