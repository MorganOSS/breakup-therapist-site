Affirming Care

Therapy that already understands the context

You deserve a therapist who understands the layered realities of queer relationships and identity without needing an explanation first. This is affirming, judgment-free space to work through relationship endings, relationship patterns, and everything tangled up in both.

That might mean navigating a breakup within a small or close-knit community, processing a relationship that didn't have family support, or untangling identity questions that got wrapped up in the relationship itself. Whatever brought you here, you don't have to start by educating your therapist.

Who This Is For

You might be here because —

  • You're LGBTQ+ and navigating a breakup, divorce, or relationship pattern you want support with
  • You want a therapist who doesn't need queerness explained to them
  • You're processing a relationship ending that intersects with identity, family, or community dynamics
  • You want the same depth of IFS and attachment work, in a space built for you specifically
What Sessions Look Like

The work itself draws on the same tools — IFS, attachment work, grief support — applied to your specific relationship and identity. Nothing about your experience needs a disclaimer or a preamble here.

Quick Answers

Common questions about this service.

Do I need to identify a specific way to work with you?

No. This space is for anyone across the LGBTQ+ spectrum navigating relationship endings or patterns — however you identify.

Will I have to explain basic queer experiences before we can get to the real work?

No — that's the point of affirming care. We start from a shared understanding, not a 101 explanation.

You don't have to have it figured out to reach out.
